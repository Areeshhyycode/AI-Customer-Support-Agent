ACME LEATHER CO. — CUSTOMER FAQ

ABOUT US
Acme Leather Co. is a Pakistani brand based in Lahore, specializing in handcrafted leather goods. Founded in 2018, we ship across Pakistan and to 25 countries worldwide. Our products are made from genuine cowhide and buffalo leather sourced from certified tanneries in Sialkot.

---

Q1. WHAT IS YOUR SHIPPING POLICY?
We offer free standard shipping on all domestic orders above PKR 5,000. For orders below this amount, a flat shipping fee of PKR 250 applies. Standard delivery within Pakistan takes 3-5 business days. Express delivery (1-2 days) is available in Lahore, Karachi, and Islamabad for an additional PKR 500. We ship via TCS, Leopards Courier, and M&P Express.

Q2. DO YOU SHIP INTERNATIONALLY?
Yes, we ship to 25 countries including USA, UK, UAE, Saudi Arabia, Canada, Australia, and most of Europe. International shipping takes 10-15 business days and is handled by DHL Express. Shipping costs are calculated at checkout based on weight and destination. Customers are responsible for any customs duties or import taxes in their country.

Q3. WHAT IS YOUR RETURN POLICY?
We accept returns within 30 days of delivery. Items must be unused, in original packaging, and with all tags attached. To initiate a return, email returns@acmeleather.pk with your order number. Return shipping is free for defective items; for change-of-mind returns, customer pays return shipping. Refunds are processed within 7 business days of receiving the returned item.

Q4. WHAT PAYMENT METHODS DO YOU ACCEPT?
We accept the following payment methods: Credit and debit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), JazzCash, EasyPaisa, Bank transfer (HBL, MCB, UBL, Allied Bank), and Cash on Delivery. COD is available only in major cities (Lahore, Karachi, Islamabad, Rawalpindi, Faisalabad, Multan, Peshawar). COD orders have an additional PKR 100 handling fee.

Q5. HOW CAN I TRACK MY ORDER?
Once your order ships, you will receive a tracking number via email and SMS. You can track your order at acmeleather.pk/track using your order number, or directly on the courier's website (TCS, Leopards, or M&P). Tracking updates appear within 24 hours of dispatch. For DHL international orders, tracking is available at dhl.com.

Q6. WHAT IS YOUR WARRANTY?
All our products come with a 1-year warranty against manufacturing defects. The warranty covers stitching, hardware, and material defects. It does not cover normal wear and tear, water damage, or damage from misuse. To claim warranty, email support@acmeleather.pk with photos of the defect and your order number.

Q7. CAN I CANCEL OR MODIFY MY ORDER?
Orders can be cancelled or modified within 4 hours of placement, before they enter our packaging stage. After 4 hours, modifications are not possible. To cancel, email cancel@acmeleather.pk or call our hotline immediately. If the order has already shipped, you may return it under our standard return policy.

Q13. HOW DO I CARE FOR MY LEATHER PRODUCTS?
To maintain your leather goods:
- Keep away from direct sunlight and heat
- Avoid contact with water; if wet, air dry naturally (do not use a hair dryer)
- Apply leather conditioner every 3-6 months
- Store in dust bags when not in use
- For deep cleaning, use only leather-specific cleaners
We also offer free professional cleaning once a year for orders above PKR 15,000.

Q16. ARE YOUR PRODUCTS ETHICALLY SOURCED?
Yes. Our leather is sourced from tanneries in Sialkot that are certified by the Leather Working Group (LWG) for environmental compliance. We do not use exotic or endangered animal hides. All our artisans are paid fair wages, work in safe conditions, and receive medical insurance. We are working towards full traceability of our supply chain by 2027.

Q17. WHAT IF MY ORDER ARRIVES DAMAGED?
If your order arrives damaged or defective, contact us within 48 hours of delivery at support@acmeleather.pk with:
1. Order number
2. Photos showing the damage
3. Photos of the packaging
We will arrange a free pickup and either replace the item or issue a full refund within 5 business days.
