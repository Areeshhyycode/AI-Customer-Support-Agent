ABOUT ACME LEATHER CO.
Acme Leather Co. is a Pakistani brand based in Lahore, specializing in handcrafted leather goods. Founded in 2018, we ship across Pakistan and to 25 countries worldwide. Our products are made from genuine full-grain cowhide and buffalo leather sourced from Leather Working Group certified tanneries in Sialkot.

====================================================
ACCOUNTS & ORDERING
====================================================

Q. DO I NEED AN ACCOUNT TO SHOP?
You can browse all products without an account. To place an order and track it, create a free account by clicking "Sign up" at the top of the site. You only need your name, email, and a password (at least 6 characters).

Q. HOW DO I PLACE AN ORDER?
Browse the Shop page, add items to your cart, and proceed to checkout. You will need to be logged in to complete a purchase. You will receive an order confirmation by email.

Q. CAN I CANCEL OR MODIFY MY ORDER?
Orders can be cancelled or modified within 4 hours of placement, before they enter our packaging stage. After 4 hours, modifications are not possible. To cancel, email cancel@acmeleather.pk immediately. If the order has already shipped, you may return it under our standard return policy.

Q. I FORGOT MY PASSWORD. WHAT DO I DO?
Use the "Forgot password" link on the login page to receive a reset email. If you do not receive it within 10 minutes, check your spam folder or contact support@acmeleather.pk.

====================================================
SHIPPING
====================================================

Q. WHAT IS YOUR SHIPPING POLICY?
We offer free standard shipping on all domestic orders above PKR 5,000. For orders below PKR 5,000, a flat shipping fee of PKR 250 applies. Standard delivery within Pakistan takes 3-5 business days. We ship via TCS, Leopards Courier, and M&P Express.

Q. DO YOU OFFER EXPRESS DELIVERY?
Yes. Express delivery (1-2 business days) is available in Lahore, Karachi, and Islamabad for an additional PKR 500.

Q. DO YOU SHIP INTERNATIONALLY?
Yes, we ship to 25 countries including USA, UK, UAE, Saudi Arabia, Canada, Australia, and most of Europe. International shipping takes 10-15 business days via DHL Express. Customers are responsible for any customs duties or import taxes in their country.

Q. HOW CAN I TRACK MY ORDER?
Once your order ships, you will receive a tracking number by email and SMS. Track your order from your account dashboard, or directly on the courier's website. Tracking updates appear within 24 hours of dispatch.

====================================================
RETURNS & WARRANTY
====================================================

Q. WHAT IS YOUR RETURN POLICY?
We accept returns within 30 days of delivery. Items must be unused, in original packaging, with all tags attached. To start a return, email returns@acmeleather.pk with your order number. Return shipping is free for defective items; for change-of-mind returns, the customer pays return shipping. Refunds are processed within 7 business days of receiving the returned item.

Q. WHAT IS YOUR WARRANTY?
All products come with a 1-year warranty against manufacturing defects, covering stitching, hardware, and material defects. It does not cover normal wear and tear, water damage, or misuse. To claim warranty, email support@acmeleather.pk with photos of the defect and your order number.

Q. WHAT IF MY ORDER ARRIVES DAMAGED?
Contact us within 48 hours of delivery at support@acmeleather.pk with your order number and photos of the damage and packaging. We will arrange a free pickup and either replace the item or issue a full refund within 5 business days.

Q. ARE CUSTOMIZED PRODUCTS RETURNABLE?
No. Customized or monogrammed items are made to order and cannot be returned unless they arrive defective.

CUSTOMIZATION & SERVICES
====================================================

Q. DO YOU OFFER PRODUCT CUSTOMIZATION?
Yes, on wallets, belts, and bags. Monogram embossing (3 characters) costs PKR 500 extra. Custom color from 12 shades costs PKR 1,000 extra. Bespoke made-to-order designs start at PKR 8,000 extra. Customized orders take an additional 7-10 business days.

Q. DO YOU HAVE BULK OR CORPORATE DISCOUNTS?
Yes. 5-10 items: 10% off. 11-25 items: 15% off. 26-50 items: 20% off. 51+ items: 25% off plus free embossing. Email corporate@acmeleather.pk for a quote. Bulk orders take 2-4 weeks.

Q. DO YOU HAVE A LOYALTY PROGRAM?
Yes, "Acme Rewards" is free to join. Earn 1 point per PKR 100 spent; 100 points equal a PKR 500 discount. Members get early access to new collections, member-only sales, free birthday-month embossing, and priority support.

Q. HOW DO I CARE FOR MY LEATHER PRODUCTS?
Keep leather away from direct sunlight and heat. Avoid water; if wet, air dry naturally without a hair dryer. Apply leather conditioner every 3-6 months. Store in dust bags when not in use. We offer free professional cleaning once a year for orders above PKR 15,000.
