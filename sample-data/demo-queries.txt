DEMO QUERIES FOR VIDEO RECORDING

Use these in your demo video. They cover different query types — perfect for showing the AI's range.

---

✅ DIRECT ANSWERS (AI should answer confidently from doc)

1. What is your shipping policy?
2. Do you ship to the UK?
3. How can I track my order?
4. What payment methods do you accept?
5. What is your warranty policy?
6. How do I care for leather products?
7. Where is your office located?
8. Do you offer gift wrapping?

---

🧠 SLIGHTLY HARDER (AI must combine info or infer)

9. I'm in Karachi, when will my order arrive?
10. Can I pay with cash if I'm ordering a wallet for PKR 3,000?
11. I bought a belt 2 weeks ago and it has a defective buckle — what should I do?
12. I want to order 30 wallets for my company employees, what's the discount?
13. Do you have a store I can visit in Islamabad?

---

🙋 HANDOFF / TICKET TRIGGERS (info not in doc — should escalate)

14. Who is the CEO of Acme Leather?
15. Can I get a refund without a receipt?
16. Do you make leather shoes? (not in product list)
17. I want to talk to a human agent

---

💬 CONVERSATION MEMORY TEST (ask in order)

18. What's your return policy?
19. How long does it take?  ← AI should remember "return" from previous Q
20. And what if I'm international?  ← AI should still be in return-policy context

---

🎬 RECOMMENDED VIDEO SEQUENCE (for 60-sec demo):

1. "What is your shipping policy?" → shows working RAG
2. "I'm in Karachi, when will my order arrive?" → shows AI inference
3. "Who is your CEO?" → shows handoff + ticket creation
4. "I want to talk to a human" → explicit escalation
5. Then go to admin → show tickets created

This sequence demos: retrieval, reasoning, handoff, and ticketing — the 4 core features.
